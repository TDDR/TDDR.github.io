About Me

Who is TDDR?

Tim Roberts is a military veteran starting on a new path. Determination, adaptability, and an ability to see the big picture are just some of the traits that have allowed for Mr.Roberts to maintain a 4.0 GPA earing a honorary bachelor of Software Development.

In his free time, Mr. Roberts enjoys going for long walk with his dog, playing fetch with his cat, exercising and making health conscious choices. I also enjoy cooking, fermenting food, and sharing that food with friends and family.

During his time in the military Cpl.Roberts was deployed to Afghanistan, provided security for the 2010 Winter olympics, and was about to be promoted to the rank of MCpl (middle management) before letting his contract expire. After leaving the Army Tim spent a few years installing and servicing HVAC equipment. He did not see a future in that trade though so at the age of 30, Tim decided to try and make a better future for himself and started a four year degree program at Seneca Collage.
