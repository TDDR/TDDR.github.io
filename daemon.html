Sockets and Security

Summary

This was a project for a professional level course called "Sockets and Security with Python". Our group had to come up with an idea of how to plant malware in the form of a deamon that we create and have it extract information for us.

We decided to run a scenario that had us acting as a disgruntled ex-employee of some big company. Having insider knowledge, we created a fake lottery prediction algorithm and targeted a few weak links that we knew to play in the company lotto pool. Wanting to win big, our target would download our malware and once they ran it to find out next weeks winning numbers our deamon would set up shop in the background awaiting our command.
